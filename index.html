📊 ATCF Measurement: From Theory to Evidence

Each ATCF component is measured using validated biomarkers and behavioral assessments.

A' (Autobiographical)

What: Coherent sense of identity over time

Measured via: Life Story Interview, self-concept clarity scale, narrative coherence

Evidence: McAdams (2001), Chandler et al. (2003)

T' (Thermodynamic)

What: Material security, physiological stress

Measured via: Cortisol levels, allostatic load, financial strain, sleep quality

Evidence: Sapolsky (2017), McEwen (2007), Evans & Schamberg (2009)

C' (Coherent Future)

What: Ability to plan for future states

Measured via: Future Consequences scale, time perspective, delay discounting

Evidence: Mullainathan & Shafir (2013), Shah et al. (2012)

F' (Free Will)

What: Override impulses, resist pressure

Measured via: Stroop test, ego depletion, executive function

Evidence: Baumeister et al. (1998), Vaughan (1996)

⚠️ Critical: ATCF values in scenarios are based on empirical studies showing measured impairment. Not arbitrary!
